ASU Racing Team

ASU Racing team is an organization in ASU that contains many teams like Formula Student UK , Shell Echo Marathon ( Urban and Prototype ) , EVER , AUV and ROV.
its mission is to teach the students the technical and non-technical skills to reduce the gap between them and the market and to participate in many competitions like formula , echo shell and mate-competition.
